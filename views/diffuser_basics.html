Basics

Occupant comfort is a critical consideration in HVAC design when it comes to ensuring occupant productivity and reducing hot/cold calls. Selecting and sizing the outlet diffusers is thus an important step in providing a comfortable environment for occupants. Outlet diffusers can be selected based on architectural requirements, noise criteria, distance of throw, airflow, etc. Location of the supply diffuser and return grille is also important. Refer to the ‘Selecting air-distribution outlets: Designing for comfort’ and ‘Designing Air-Distribution Systems to Maximize Comfort’ articles by David John for ASHRAE Journal for more details on diffuser selection and sizing.
