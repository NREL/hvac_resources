Basics

Occupant comfort is a critical piece of HVAC design when it comes to ensuring occupant productivity and reducing hot/cold calls. Selecting and sizing the outlet diffusers thus is an important step in providing a comfortable environment for the occupants. Outlet diffusers can be selected based on architectural requirements, noise criteria, distance of throw, air flow etc. Location of the supply diffuser and return grille is also important. Refer ‘Selecting air-distribution outlets: Designing for comfort’ article by David John for more details on diffuser selection and sizing.

Each diffuser type has a different pressure drop which also needs to be taken into account when selecting the diffuser. The table below gives a comparison between the performance parameters for commonly used diffuser types.
