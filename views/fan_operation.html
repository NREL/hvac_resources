Operation and Training Resources

Designing and installing high performing fan systems is a good first step, but to ensure intended performance is obtained and sustained, system commissioning and ongoing maintenance is required. Testing and balancing of air systems also matches installed equipment to design intent and reduces energy use by improperly balanced systems.

  • Field test results detailing control strategies for reducing energy in RTUs. “Advanced Rooftop Control (ARC) Retrofit: Field-Test Results” Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, PNNL-22656.
  • Different static pressure reset schedules are reviewed and compared. Liu, M., et al. “Impacts of Static Pressure Reset on VAV System Air Leakage, Fan Power, and Thermal Energy – Part I: Theoretical Model and Simulation”. DigitalCommons@University of Nebraska-Lincoln, pp. 1-13.
