Basics

The piping network is one of the important and expensive components in a centralized cold/hot water or steam distribution system. These pipes are generally buried underground and need to supply the working fluid to the individual buildings at the appropriate pressure and temperature. Pipe failures can critically affect the building operation at the facility. Pipe materials commonly used for chilled and hot water applications are shown in the table below:

Pipe material Service
Carbon steel CHW, HHW
Type K copper CHW, HHW
Ductile iron CHW, HHW
PVC CHW
HDPE CHW

CHW= chilled water, HHW= heating hot water
