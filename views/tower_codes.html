Energy Codes and Standards

Cooling tower commissioning is done in-line with the standard published by ASME (PTC-23) or Cooling Technology Institute (CTI- ATC 105). The test parameters include allowable range for flow rate, heat load, fan power, Wet Bulb Temperature, Dry Bulb Temperature, wind velocity, barometric pressure, oil substances, total dissolved solids etc. For additional information about thermal performance testing, refer to the Thermal Performance Testing page on SPX Cooling Technologies site.

In critical projects, on-site commissioning of open and closed circuit cooling tower is done to ensure integration of individual components to the system level sequence of operation. Another important reason to do field commissioning is to ensure the unit meets the required capacity. For detailed information on testing sensors, actuation and sequencing, refer to Functional Testing for Condensers and Cooling Towers.
