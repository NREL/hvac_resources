Basics

Pump is a mechanical device used to move fluid. Their typical application in a central plant is to move chilled/hot water and condenser water through the loop. Booster or secondary pumps are sometimes used in individual buildings to supplement the pump at the central plant. Most commonly used pumps for central plant applications are centrifugal type.

Source: Photos by Dennis Schroeder, NREL 38460, 38461

Pumps are broadly classified as end suction and inline pumps. For further classification of both these pump types refer the Pump Handbook by Grundfos Industry.
