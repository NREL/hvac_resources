Pump maintenance

Why is pump maintenance important?

If the pump is not operating near its Best efficiency point (BEP), the power consumed will be high and disproportionate to the flow rate. Monitoring is recommended to identify such inefficiencies.


What is preventive, predictive and periodic maintenance of pumps?

· Preventive action measures are those which are taken to prevent a pump from failing. It consists of coupling alignment, lubrication and seal maintenance and replacement.

· Predictive action measures are those that help cover for unexpected equipment outage. Vibration analysis, motor current signature analysis, lubrication oil analysis, periodic efficiency testing are methods suggested under predictive actions.

· Periodic maintenance checklist consists of checking for leakage from packing and mechanical seals; monitoring condition of bearings, evaluating motor/pump alignment and checking motor condition.
