Operation and Training Resources

Proper operation of a chiller includes periodic maintenance and ongoing monitoring of performance. Some steps suggested to ensure efficient chiller operation are:

Annual chiller inspection, check for:

  • Refrigerant and air leaks (Check low-pressure systems for excess air and high-pressure systems for appropriate refrigerant levels)
  • Compressor operating pressures
  • Motor voltage and amps (Look out for increases in current draw
  • Electrical starters, contactors and relays
  • Evaporator and condenser tube bundle cleanliness and integrity

Performance Indicators
  • Condenser and evaporator entering and leaving water temperature and flow
  • Chiller load profile
  • Chiller energy performance (kW/Ton)
  • Oil level and pressure

For additional information on each of the above efficiency measure, refer to Section 9 in the Operations and Maintenance Best Practices guide published by U.S. DOE, ANSI/ASHRAE/ACCA Standard 180-2012, Standard Practice for Inspection and Maintenance of Commercial Building HVAC Systems, and Chapter 6 in the Energy-Related Best Practices sourcebook published by Iowa State.
