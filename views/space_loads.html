SPACE LOADS

The primary purpose of HVAC systems is to provide healthy and comfortable conditioning of the occupied spaces in buildings. HVAC systems are designed and operated to meet the thermal loads of all the occupied spaces in buildings. It makes logical sense that minimizing and managing the space thermal loads is the first place to start when looking for energy savings.
