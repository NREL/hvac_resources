Operation and Training Resources

Periodic boiler evaluation is required to ensure that the boiler is operating as designed. The functional testing guide for boilers gives detailed guidance on steps to perform a functional test of boilers. It also has estimates of time required to carry out the testing and links to various checklists for boiler commissioning.

Example of actions that need special attention during the testing process include

  • Verifying proper boiler staging under normal operation, failure and emergency modes. Proper coordination between boiler staging and load variation will save energy
  • Verifying boiler starts/stops with the time delay specified during design
  • Verifying all control loops stabilize within a reasonable time (typically 2-5 minutes) after a significant load change

Refer the Boiler Tune-ups factsheet by the EPA for a detailed list of boiler systems energy efficiency-measures.
